# The Evolution of Studio Trigger

Studio Trigger has become synonymous with bold, energetic animation that pushes boundaries. But their journey from Gainax's ashes to industry leaders is a fascinating story of creative vision and technical excellence.

## The Gainax Legacy

Founded by former Gainax employees, Trigger inherited a legacy of experimental storytelling and dynamic animation. But they took these foundations and evolved them for a new generation.

## Kill la Kill: The Breakthrough

Their first major work, Kill la Kill, announced Trigger's arrival with explosive energy. The show's distinctive style and unapologetic excess became their calling card.

## Technical Innovation

Trigger's approach to animation combines traditional hand-drawn techniques with modern digital tools. Their sakuga sequences are legendary, featuring fluid motion and creative camera work.

## Recent Works

From Little Witch Academia to Cyberpunk: Edgerunners, Trigger continues to evolve while maintaining their core identity. Each project showcases their commitment to visual storytelling.

## Impact on the Industry

Trigger has influenced a generation of animators and studios. Their success proves that bold creative vision can thrive in the modern anime landscape.
