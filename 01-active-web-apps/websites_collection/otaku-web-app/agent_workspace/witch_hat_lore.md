# Witch Hat Atelier - Campaign Setting & Lore Book

## Overview
*Witch Hat Atelier* is a fantasy series where magic is not an innate genetic gift, but an intricate, accessible art form rooted in geometry, precision, and rigorous study. The world is divided between the "Known" (witches who practice the secret art) and the "Unknowing" (ordinary people who believe magic is an inherent miracle).

## The Magic System: Drawn Magic
In this setting, magic is performed entirely through drawing **Casting Seals**—precise, geometric patterns that manipulate the laws of nature using specialized magical ink and quills.

### Anatomy of a Spell
A casting seal consists of three core components:
1. **Sigil (The Core):** Placed at the center of the seal, this dictates the elemental foundation (Fire, Water, Wind, Earth, Light, etc.) and the core nature of the spell.
2. **Signs (The Modifiers):** Symbols surrounding the sigil that define the parameters of the spell. These control direction, scale, intensity, shape, and behavior (e.g., launching as a projectile, floating gently, or raining down).
3. **Ring (The Trigger):** The outer bounding circle that binds the seal. The magic is dormant until this ring is perfectly closed. This allows witches to "pre-load" spells on fabric, paper, or objects and activate them later by completing the circle.

### The Golden Rule of Magic
Because magic can be learned by *anyone*, it is highly regulated. To prevent the wars and atrocities of the past, the use of magic on the human body (healing, transformation, memory manipulation) is **strictly forbidden**.

## World Lore & Factions

### The Pact and The Great Hall
After ancient magical wars nearly destroyed the world, a treaty called **The Pact** was established. The Great Hall enforces the Pact, ensuring that the Unknowing never learn the truth about magic. If an Unknowing discovers the secret, their memories are forcefully erased—a traumatic process that removes the individual's memories entirely, not just their knowledge of magic. Witches who follow the Great Hall wear pointed hats, symbolizing order.

### The Knights Moralis
The law enforcement and magical police force of the Great Hall. Their primary duty is to hunt down rogue magic users, punish those who use forbidden magic, and erase the memories of Unknowing who learn too much.

### The Brimmed Caps
A rogue faction of witches who reject the Great Hall's restrictions. They believe that magic should be free and openly practice forbidden arts, including body modification and healing. They wear wide-brimmed hats and are actively trying to overthrow the Pact, seeing the Great Hall's hoarding of magic as a travesty.

## Key Characters

### Coco
The protagonist, an optimistic and determined girl born to an Unknowing dressmaker. Coco always dreamed of magic but thought it was impossible for her. After accidentally turning her mother to stone using a forbidden picture book, she becomes an apprentice witch to reverse the spell. She is ignorant of many magical norms but possesses incredible creativity and talent.

### Qifrey
Coco's mentor and a highly skilled, patient witch who runs his own Atelier. While he genuinely cares for Coco and his other apprentices (Agott, Tetia, and Richeh), he has a dark ulterior motive. He is using Coco's connection to the forbidden picture book to track down the Brimmed Caps. Qifrey lost an eye and parts of his memory to the Brimmed Caps in his past and is obsessed with finding them.

## Major Plot Hook: The Forbidden Curse
**The Hook:** A young, ordinary character (like Coco) is gifted or purchases a mysterious, seemingly blank picture book and an ink set from a masked stranger (a Brimmed Cap). Upon witnessing a real witch cast a spell, they realize the book contains Casting Seals. 
Attempting to practice the shapes, they accidentally draw a piece of forbidden magic, resulting in a disaster (e.g., a loved one being petrified or a localized spatial collapse). A skilled witch intervenes, saving them, but now the character must be hidden from the Knights Moralis. They are taken in as a secret apprentice to learn enough magic to reverse the curse, all while being hunted by both the magical police and the sinister Brimmed Caps who want to use them for their own ends.
